To clear direct formatting, like text highlighting and such, for 
entire page:

1. Press Ctrl+A to select the whole text
2. Press Ctrl+M to clear direct formatting

i.e.:
  Ctrl + A
  Ctrl + M


--help.libreoffice.org/Common/Undoing_Direct_Formatting_for_a_Document
